# PulsePoint: Healthcare & Risk Analysis Dashboard

A dynamic, interactive data visualization tool built to explore hospital performance and patient health metrics—focusing on clinical outcomes, departmental efficiency, and heart attack risk prediction.

## 1. Short Description / Purpose
The **PulsePoint Dashboard** is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to help healthcare providers monitor patient vitals and hospital operations in real-time. The dashboard focuses on identifying high-risk clinical patterns and predicting heart attack probability to assist medical staff in prioritizing emergency care. This tool is intended for use by hospital administrators, clinical researchers, and healthcare professionals who seek to optimize patient outcomes and resource allocation.

## 2. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping patient health records.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, risk scoring logic, and dynamic KPIs.
* 📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established between patient demographics, clinical vitals, and hospital departments to enable cross-filtering.
* 🐍 **Python (Scikit-learn / Pandas)** – Utilized for predictive modeling to classify heart attack risk levels.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.jpg` for dashboard previews.

## 3. Data Source
**Source:** Anonymized Healthcare Clinical Dataset.
Data on patient demographics (age, gender), clinical vitals (blood pressure, cholesterol, heart rate), and hospital operational records (departmental load, admission dates, and recovery times).

## 4. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem
The healthcare industry faces a critical need for rapid diagnostic insights to combat cardiovascular diseases, a leading cause of mortality. Hospital administrators and clinicians often lack an intuitive way to identify high-risk patients before a critical event occurs.
Key questions such as:
* Which patient demographics are at the highest risk for heart attacks?
* Which hospital departments are currently over-capacity?
* How do clinical vitals like cholesterol and BP correlate with patient risk levels?
... are difficult to answer quickly with raw medical records.

### • Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:
* Enables healthcare staff to explore patient risk factors globally.
* Supports clinical decisions such as emergency prioritization and preventative care.
* Uncovers trends in hospital performance, patient recovery, and departmental efficiency.

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals
* **Key Health KPIs (Top Header)**
    * Total Patients Screened, High-Risk Percentage, Average Recovery Time, and Average Cholesterol.
* **Risk Level Filter Panel**
    * An interactive slicer lets users filter all visuals by risk categories: High, Medium, or Low.
* **Risk by Age Group (Bar Chart)**
    * Ranks age brackets by the frequency of high-risk clinical markers to identify vulnerable demographics.
* **Correlation of Vitals (Scatter Plot)**
    * Displays the relationship between Blood Pressure and Heart Rate to spot outliers.
* **Departmental Load (Stacked Bar Chart)**
    * Shows patient distribution across Cardiology, ICU, Emergency, and General Wards.
* **Heart Attack Probability (Gauge Chart)**
    * Displays the average prediction score generated by the integrated machine learning model.

### • Business Impact & Insights
* **Emergency Prioritization**: Identify and treat high-risk patients immediately, reducing response times.
* **Resource Optimization**: Identify under-resourced departments and reallocate staff or beds based on patient load.
* **Preventative Care**: Target specific high-risk age groups for preventative health campaigns.
* **Operational Efficiency**: Streamlines the data-to-insight process for faster hospital reporting.
